Welcome to The Willows Dental Practice

The Willows Dental Practice is located in the village of Belton in rural North Lincolnshire just off junction 2 of the M180. The practice has been open since 2008 and is owned and run by Dr Andrew Newton. The practice is run on an independant basis and offers a range of routine family dentistry, orthodontics including Invisalign® invisible braces, cosmetic dentistry, all at affordable prices. Denplan monthly payment plans are also available.

Covid information

To all our patients. This is a challenging time for all of us. Our priority is the safety of our patients and dental team. The surgery has now reopened, however inline with current guidance there are restrictions as to the type and availability of dental treatment. We are steadily working through the backlog of appointments and will endeavour to contact the patients who had their appointments cancelled during the lockdown closure. Please DO NOT attend the surgery in person unless asked to do so. Our usual opening hours have resumed but due to the extra time and cleaning which now has to carried out after each patient we are having to see fewer patients each day. Outside these hours if you have a dental emergency such as facial swelling, pain that cannot be controlled by the use of pain relief, trauma following an accident, bleeding following an extraction, please contact NHS Direct on 111. If you are a Denplan patient please contact the Denplan helpline on 0800844999 Andrew & all the team

Dr Andrew Newton

BDS (U.Sheff'd)

Andrew qualified from the University of Sheffield School of Clinical Dentistry in 1988 with the degree of Bachelor of Dental Surgery. Andrew has full General Dental Council Registration no 63683

His first position was in a practice in Doncaster town centre. He moved to the Westwoodside practice in 1990 and subsequently bought it in 1992 and became principal dentist. Andrew set up this purpose built practice site at The Willows in Belton in 2008 to run as an independant practice and has now passed over control of the Westwoodside practice so that he can concentrate all his efforts at The Willows and carry out the quality care the patients deserve.

Mrs Lyn Foster

Our Hygienist

Lyn qualified as a dental hygienist in the army and has worked for many years in general practice. She is fully registered with the GDC number 2139. Lyn works part time at the practice and is available on a Monday morning and afternoon by appointment on a private basis. Lyn also works at other practices in Scunthorpe and Goole.

Our Nursing Team

Our nursing team are dedicated to assisting the dentists to provide you with quality dental care. Our nurses all take part in continual professional development training to keep their knowledge and skills up to date. Some of our nurses also have further qualifications in dental radiography to allow them to take x-rays. Our nursing team are all fully qualified and registered with the General Dental Council and all take an active part in Continual Professional Development as required by the General Dental Council.
